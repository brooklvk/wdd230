Site Plan Target Audience Design

The target audience of this chamber of commerce site will be business owners who live in the Manassa area. They will mostly be owners of agricultural businesses who are looking to expand their operation and make new business connections.

Persona 1: Male, 50-70, owner of a cattle ranch. Looking to expand business by buying land and equipment, making new contacts, and improving the quality of the land and cattle.

Persona 2: Female, 20-40, owner of local market/ business. Looking to meet new people in order to advertise better and improve business systems and skills.

Scenario 1: Persona 1 comes to the site looking for resources for where to buy equipment locally. Needs an easy-to-find resource section on the website.

Scenario 2: Persona 1 comes to the site looking for a networking event with other ranchers in the area. Needs an easy-to-find event section on the website.

Scenario 3: Persona 2 comes to the site looking for a local farmer's market event. Needs an easy-to-find event section on the website.

Scenario 4: Persona 2 comes to the site looking for a way to advertise her business. Needs to find an advertising event or resources.

Scenario 5: Persona 2 comes to the site wondering who she can contact to ask a specific question about the chamber. Needs to find a contact page on the website.
